BUILD 2026

The AI Builder Atlas

“Build 2026 was overwhelming. There was so much innovation it became easy to miss the larger narrative — how all of these pieces actually come together to productionise AI agents. This page is an attempt at interpretation of that narrative.”

One coherent throughline across the announcements: pick your intelligence, ground it, give it tools, run it safely, and place it on the right compute. Five builder layers, eleven sample tracks, one assembly line.
