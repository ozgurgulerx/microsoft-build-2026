# Sample 11: Discovery Research Agent

Goal: shape an agentic research workflow that is evidence-backed, tool-aware, and reviewable.

## Builder Question

How do I make agentic research useful without letting it become speculative or unreviewed?

## Planned Shape

- Synthetic scientific or engineering scenario.
- Hypothesis, evidence, tool-use, and reviewer-checkpoint loop.
- Source and artifact registry.
- Risk register for unsupported claims and unsafe automation.
- Review handoff from agent output to human owner.
- Clear distinction between narrative demo and production R&D workflow.
